About Us

The animal Welfare is an online, searchable database of animals who need homes. It is also a directory of nearly 11,000 animal shelters and adoption organizations across the U.S., Canada and Mexico. Organizations maintain their own home pages and available-pet databases.

Our mission is to use Internet technology and the resources so we acheive to:

  1. Increase public awareness of the availability of high-quality adoptable pets
  2. Increase the overall effectiveness of pet adoption programs.
  3. Elevate the status of pets to that of family member
